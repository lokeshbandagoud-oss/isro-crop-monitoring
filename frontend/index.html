🛰 Project Overview

The ISRO AI Crop Monitoring Platform is an AI-powered precision agriculture system that combines Google Earth Engine, Sentinel-2 Satellite Imagery, and Artificial Intelligence to monitor crop health and provide smart irrigation recommendations. Farmers can search their location, draw their field boundary, and receive field-specific analysis through an interactive GIS dashboard.

The platform calculates NDVI and NDMI indices to evaluate vegetation health and crop moisture. Using these indices, the system estimates crop type, growth stage, moisture stress, water balance, and irrigation requirements to support precision farming and sustainable agriculture.

📐 Indices & Formulae

NDVI (Crop Health)

NDVI = (NIR − Red) / (NIR + Red)

NIR = Band 8
Red = Band 4


NDMI (Crop Moisture)

NDMI = (NIR − SWIR) / (NIR + SWIR)

NIR = Band 8
SWIR = Band 11
